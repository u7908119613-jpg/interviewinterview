# Generic Question Bank Template

> This is a template, not a personal question bank. Replace fictional placeholders with source-backed material.

---

## E · Experience

| question_id | generic question | PI-specific adaptation | evidence needed | earned-secret prompt |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| E1 | Tell me about a research project you are proud of. | Which prior project best shows readiness for this PI's method or problem? | one concrete project, decision, result | What did you learn that is not obvious from the final output? |
| E2 | Describe a challenge or failure in your research. | What failure mode resembles a risk in this PhD project? | constraint, failed attempt, repair | What changed in your method after the failure? |
| E3 | What methods have you used, and why? | Which method choice maps to the PI's evidence standards? | method, alternative, tradeoff | Why was the rejected alternative not enough? |

## W · Working Style

| question_id | generic question | PI-specific adaptation | evidence needed | earned-secret prompt |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| W1 | How do you work with a supervisor? | What coordination rhythm would reduce risk for this PI? | meeting / feedback / autonomy example | When do you escalate versus continue independently? |
| W2 | How do you handle ambiguous problems? | How would you scope an uncertain first-year task? | ambiguity, first step, validation loop | What early signal tells you the path is wrong? |
| W3 | How do you learn a new method? | How would you ramp up on a method central to this project? | prior learning curve | How do you avoid shallow tool adoption? |

## P · Passion

| question_id | generic question | PI-specific adaptation | evidence needed | earned-secret prompt |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| P1 | Why this PhD project? | What exact open question in the project pulls your prior work forward? | bridge from prior work to project | What is the unresolved problem you cannot stop thinking about? |
| P2 | Why this PI or lab? | Which part of the PI's work changes your research plan? | paper / method / lab context | What did you notice that a generic applicant might miss? |
| P3 | Where do you see your research going? | What first-year question is both useful to the lab and credible for you? | near-term research plan | What would you test first, and why? |

## S · Synthesis

| question_id | generic question | PI-specific adaptation | evidence needed | earned-secret prompt |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| S1 | Give a short self-introduction. | Can you connect background -> PI problem -> first contribution in 60 seconds? | concise narrative | What is the one-sentence research bridge? |
| S2 | What would you do in your first six months? | What low-risk, source-backed first step fits the PI's project? | project understanding, method plan | What would you not do first, and why? |
| S3 | What questions do you have for us? | What question shows real engagement without pretending insider knowledge? | PI paper / project detail | What answer would change your preparation plan? |
